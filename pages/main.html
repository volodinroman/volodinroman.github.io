About me

I have been working in the entertainment industry as a Sr. Technical Artist/Pipeline Engineer since 2013. I am the one who combines art and technology to provide technical solutions for artists' needs. At the end of the day, I'm here to help artists deliver results and make a difference in this industry.

In 2005, I began my academic journey at Komsomolsk-on-Amur State Technical University (KNAST), studying Computational Technologies, specialising in Software Engineering, Web Development and CAD systems. I graduated with an M.Eng. degree in Computer Graphics and Multimedia. Additionally, in 2013 I enrolled a "3D Animation" course at Seneca College in Toronto.

Since 2018, I have been teaching a Python course for artists, riggers and animators.

I see myself as a strong leader, good communicator and team player, and highly committed to my projects, while always striving to experiment and learn new technique and technologies.
